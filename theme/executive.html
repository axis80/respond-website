Executive

The Executive theme from Respond provides your business or personal site with a professional, timeless feel.  And, the built-in parallax callout on the home screen gives your site a little unexpected whimsy. With over 50 point-and-click configurations built-in to the the theme, you can quickly make the Executive theme your own without ever touching any code.

About

 Respond is an open source, responsive content management system (CMS) built on AngularJS and Polymer-based Web Components that you can use to build responsive sites. The latest version features Bootstrap 3.0, robust themes, a complete REST API, and a beautiful UI.
